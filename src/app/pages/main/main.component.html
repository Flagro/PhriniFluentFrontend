PhriniFluent 🐫

PhriniFluent is an opensource, OpenAI-powered language learning project. Phrini the camel 🐪 will be your personal assistant in this journey. Generate words of any language - From Spanish to duck language 🦆 with the use of AI. Add your own words that you want to learn, extract words that you might want to learn based on your level from texts, and many more!

You can use this website for this but we also have a telegram bot that syncs with your progress here at @PhriniFluentBot 🤖.

This project is developed by Anton Potapov github.com/Flagro and Dmitry Trofimushkin github.com/Swel1a.

Discover more about Phrini Verse at flagrotown.com!
